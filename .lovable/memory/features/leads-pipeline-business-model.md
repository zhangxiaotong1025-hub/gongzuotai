## Business Model
- Platform acquires leads from channels (抖音、百度、官网、小红书、线下、400电话)
- Customer service team cleanses leads (manual, 8 people, bottleneck)
- Cleansed leads distributed to enterprises at ¥160-250/lead
- Enterprises follow up and convert leads to sales
- **Critical gap**: After distribution, pipeline breaks — only 34% enterprises provide follow-up feedback

## Key Pain Points
1. **Rising CAC**: Lead acquisition cost increasing month over month
2. **Manual cleansing**: 100% human, expensive, inconsistent quality standards
3. **Manual distribution**: Region-based only, no intelligent matching
4. **Broken feedback loop**: Enterprises don't report back, making ROI calculation impossible
5. **No tracking**: Post-distribution visibility is zero

## Platform vs Enterprise Perspective
- Platform view: acquisition cost, cleansing pipeline, distribution efficiency, feedback rates
- Enterprise view: received leads, contact rate, conversion, satisfaction with quality

## AI Automation Priorities
- P0: AI outbound call screening, intelligent scoring, smart matching, feedback incentives
- P1: Dynamic pricing, AI creative generation, smart bidding
- P2: CPS auto-settlement

## Feedback Incentive Mechanisms
- Quality deposit (预付保障金)
- Points-for-leads (积分兑客资)
- Dynamic pricing tied to feedback rate
- AI auto-reminders for missing feedback
